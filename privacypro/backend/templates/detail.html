What does this mean?

This permission allows the app to access your sensitive data. When granted, the app can read or record this information even when you’re not actively using it.

For example, if this is a microphone permission, the app could record audio in the background. If it’s location, the app can track your movements over time.

Why review?

  • Unused permissions are a potential privacy risk.
  • Apps with permissions they don’t need can be exploited by malicious code.
  • Revoking unnecessary permissions helps minimize data collection.
